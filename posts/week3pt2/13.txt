   <p><strong>Provider In/Out network data accessibility :</strong></p> 
   <p>Whenever there is a need to visit hospitals or doctors we might take an appointment and go to the hospital/physician.We dont know wheter the doctor/physician is IN/Out network to the health insurance.</p> 
   <p>Health insurance companies charges seprate maximum out of pocket charges and diagnosis charges for Out network when compared to IN network doctors/hospitals/physicians.</p> 
   <p>We have lack of data availability to check whether the doctors/hospitals/physicians is IN network or not. If we have ability to see this data then we can choose the nearest IN network physician and get the medication.Good example is if IN network maximum out of pocket is 3k then for OUT netowrk it will be around 6K.</p> 
   <p>All of us will be benefitted if we have the availability of data to check who is IN network or not.</p>
