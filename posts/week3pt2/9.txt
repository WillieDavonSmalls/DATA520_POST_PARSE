, sans-serif;font-size: 13.0px;font-style: normal;font-weight: 400;letter-spacing: normal;orphans: 2;text-indent: 0.0px;text-transform: none;white-space: normal;widows: 2;word-spacing: 0.0px;">&nbsp;(e.g.&nbsp;</span>Samsung<span style="color: rgb(0,0,0);font-family: &quot;Open Sans&quot;
