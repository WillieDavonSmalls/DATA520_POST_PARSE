  <div class="vtbegenerated">
   <p>Here is a link to a github repo that contains a few notebooks regarding gender imbalance in Game of Thrones.&nbsp; This analysis uses the scripts from&nbsp;episodes of the Game of Thrones TV&nbsp;show.&nbsp; I like this analysis because I often heard of Game of Thrones being characterized as sexist and negative towards women, but having watched the show did not necessarily agree with those assertions.&nbsp; As the seasons progress, more and more women are seen in powerful roles.&nbsp; They exude confidence and strength.</p> 
   <p>This analysis does show that men talk more than women overall and it does show that there is an increase for females in their share of the talking as seasons progress.&nbsp; It does not necessarily address the quality of the spoken words.&nbsp; As in many things, quality can be more important than quantity.&nbsp; It does look at the most frequent words and some sentiment analysis by gender, but I think a deeper analysis of the quality of the words would be beneficial.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p> 
   <p><a href="https://github.com/anahitabahri/NLP-Final-Project" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">https://github.com/anahitabahri/NLP-Final-Project</a></p> 
   <p>&nbsp;I found this notebook using Google search terms of "github", "Game of Thrones", "nlp"&nbsp;</p>
